Privacy Policy

Application: Orbitask

Developer: Orivox Labs

Effective Date: July 3, 2026

Introduction

Orbitask is an offline task management application developed by Orivox Labs. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information.

Information We Collect

Orbitask does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All information you enter, including task descriptions, priority levels, and timestamps, is stored locally on your device.

No account registration is required.

Data Storage

All data remains on your device. Orivox Labs does not have access to your tasks and does not operate any servers that receive your data.

Permissions

Orbitask only requests permissions that are necessary for the app to function. The app does not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location.

Third-Party Services

The current version of Orbitask does not use third-party analytics or cloud services.

Future versions may display advertisements using Google AdMob. If advertisements are introduced, Google may collect certain device information in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

Orivox Labs does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Orbitask is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
